Black Back PVC Flex Banner

Black PVC Flex Banner refers to a specific type of PVC banner material that is manufactured with a black backing layer. This backing layer serves several purposes:
1. Light Blocking,
2. Enhanced Print Quality,
3. Durability and Longevity.
Black PVC Flex Banners are commonly used for applications where light penetration needs to be minimized, such as outdoor banners, billboards, signage, and backlit displays.
